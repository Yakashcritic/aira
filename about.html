About AIRA 2K26

Our Vision

AIRA 2K26 aims to be the premier technical symposium that brings together the brightest minds in artificial intelligence and machine learning. We strive to create a platform where innovation meets creativity, and ideas transform into reality.

Our vision is to inspire the next generation of tech leaders and foster a community of lifelong learners who will shape the future of AI and ML.

Department of Computer Science (AI & ML)

The Department of Computer Science (AI & ML) at SRMIST Ramapuram Campus is at the forefront of artificial intelligence education and research. Our state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty provide students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in this rapidly evolving field.

We offer comprehensive programs that cover machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and more, preparing students for the challenges of tomorrow.
